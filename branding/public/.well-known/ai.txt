# About Visir
Visir is a unified productivity platform that combines email, Slack, calendar, and task management into one intelligent timeline. Our mission is to help professionals reclaim their focus time by eliminating app switching and context loss.

# Key features
- Unified inbox for Gmail, Outlook, Slack, and Google Calendar
- AI-powered email triage and categorization
- Calendar integration with time blocking
- Task management with drag-and-drop
- Cross-platform sync (macOS, Windows, iOS, Android)
- Offline support
- Privacy-first architecture with local data processing
