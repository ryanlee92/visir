## App Store Optimization Tips

### Keywords to Include
- Productivity app
- Email management
- Task management
- Calendar app
- AI assistant
- Unified inbox
- Time blocking

### Best Practices
- Clear and concise copy
- Bullet points for readability
- User benefits emphasized
- CTA included (free trial)

### Length Limits
- iOS App Store: Maximum 4,000 characters
- Google Play Store: Maximum 4,000 characters
- Short version: 500-1,000 characters (recommended)
- Detailed version: 2,000-3,000 characters
