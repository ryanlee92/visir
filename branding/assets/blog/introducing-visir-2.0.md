# Introducing Visir 2.0: The AI Executive Assistant

Today marks a milestone in Visir's journey. We're launching Visir 2.0, our biggest update yet—and it's not just about new features. It's about fundamentally reimagining how you interact with your work.

For the past year, we've been quietly building something that goes beyond productivity tools. We've been building an AI executive assistant that doesn't just sit in the background—it actively understands your context, anticipates your needs, and helps you make decisions faster.

## The Problem We're Solving

Let's start with a question: How many times have you opened your email in the morning, only to spend the next 30 minutes trying to figure out what actually matters? You're not alone. The average knowledge worker receives 121 emails per day, and studies show it takes 23 minutes to refocus after each interruption.

The problem isn't just volume—it's fragmentation. Your important emails are mixed with newsletters. Your Slack messages are scattered across channels. Your calendar events lack context. Your tasks are disconnected from the conversations that spawned them.

We built Visir 1.0 to solve the fragmentation problem. We unified your inbox, calendar, and tasks into one timeline. But we realized that unification alone wasn't enough. You still had to manually triage, prioritize, and decide what to do next.

That's where AI comes in.

## What Makes Visir 2.0 Different

Visir 2.0 introduces an AI executive assistant that doesn't just summarize—it understands. It doesn't just suggest—it reasons. It doesn't just help—it anticipates.

### Smart Email Reading: Context Beyond Keywords

Traditional email assistants rely on keyword matching and simple rules. Visir 2.0 uses advanced language models to understand the *meaning* behind your emails, not just the words.

Here's how it works:

**Contextual Understanding**: When you receive an email about a project deadline, Visir doesn't just flag it as "urgent." It understands:
- The project's relationship to other ongoing work
- The sender's typical communication style and priorities
- Historical patterns in similar situations
- Your calendar availability and existing commitments

**Thread Intelligence**: Visir reads entire email threads, not just individual messages. It understands the conversation flow, identifies action items, and tracks decisions made across multiple exchanges.

**Sentiment and Urgency Detection**: The AI can distinguish between "FYI" emails and "I need this ASAP" requests, even when the language is subtle. It learns your communication patterns and adapts to your style.

### Intelligent Drafts: Writing That Sounds Like You

One of the most time-consuming parts of email is crafting responses. Visir 2.0 doesn't just suggest templates—it writes drafts that sound like you.

**Personalized Tone**: The AI learns your writing style from your sent emails. It matches your formality level, your typical greeting style, and even your preferred sign-off.

**Context-Aware Responses**: When drafting a reply, Visir considers:
- The original message's tone and urgency
- Your relationship with the sender
- Relevant information from your calendar and tasks
- Previous conversations and decisions

**Multi-Step Reasoning**: For complex requests, Visir can break down the response into logical sections, ensuring you address all points while maintaining coherence.

### Unified Workspace Intelligence

The real magic happens when Visir connects the dots across your entire workspace.

**Cross-Platform Awareness**: Visir understands that the Slack message from your teammate about a bug is related to the GitHub issue you starred last week, which is connected to the calendar event for the sprint review.

**Proactive Suggestions**: Instead of waiting for you to ask, Visir proactively suggests:
- "You have a meeting with Sarah in 2 hours. Here's a summary of your recent email thread."
- "This email mentions a deadline next Friday. Should I block time on your calendar?"
- "You've been discussing this project in Slack. Want me to create a task?"

**Intelligent Triage**: Your unified inbox is automatically organized by:
- **Urgency**: What needs attention now vs. later
- **Project**: Related items grouped together
- **Action Required**: Clear distinction between informational and actionable items
- **Context**: Items that relate to upcoming meetings or deadlines

## Privacy and Control

We know that AI assistants raise privacy concerns. That's why we've built Visir 2.0 with privacy at its core.

**On-Device Processing**: Sensitive operations happen locally when possible. Your emails aren't sent to external servers for processing unless you explicitly enable cloud features.

**Transparent AI**: Every AI suggestion includes an explanation. You can see why Visir categorized something as urgent, why it suggested a particular draft, or why it flagged an item for your attention.

**Full Control**: You can:
- Disable AI features entirely and use Visir as a unified workspace
- Customize AI behavior to match your preferences
- Review and edit all AI suggestions before they're applied
- Export or delete your data at any time

**No Training on Your Data**: We don't use your emails or messages to train our models. Your data stays yours.

## Getting Started

Ready to experience Visir 2.0? Here's how to get started:

1. **Update Your App**: If you're already using Visir, update to version 2.0. New users can download from our website.

2. **Enable AI Features**: When you first launch Visir 2.0, you'll see a setup wizard that walks you through enabling AI features. You can always change these settings later.

3. **Let It Learn**: Give Visir a few days to learn your patterns. The more you use it, the better it gets at understanding your preferences.

4. **Customize Your Preferences**: Visit Settings → AI to customize how Visir categorizes emails, suggests drafts, and organizes your workspace.

5. **Start Using Smart Features**: 
   - Check your Home tab for AI-generated summaries
   - Try drafting a reply using the AI suggestions
   - Let Visir organize your inbox automatically
